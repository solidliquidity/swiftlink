Frequently Asked Questions

  • How can we rely on SwiftLink for athlete endorsements?

    SwiftLink works tirelessly to ensure that all endorsements are beneficial for both athletes and brands, creating genuine value.

  • What are the fees for registering as an athlete?

    Currently, SwiftLink offers free registration for early adopters. Fees will apply for future registrations.

  • Is company registration necessary for brand partnerships?

    Yes, companies must register to access our pool of talented athletes and to ensure all partnerships are aligned and beneficial.

  • What type of brands can promote their products?

    Brands that align with our athletes' values and have a strong ethical standing are welcome to register for partnerships.

  • How do brands ensure athletes promote their products effectively?

    Athletes create promotional content in line with brand guidelines, which are reviewed by both SwiftLink and the brand before publication.
